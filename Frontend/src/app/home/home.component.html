Who are we?

We are a part of an NGO Jaljeevika. Our main focus is to provide relevant information to the farmers about FPO's. And also provide them with a Market Place where they can sell their products and get a fair amount of money against it. We want you to ask your fellow-farmer friends to join us.

What we do?

We provide relevant information to the farmers about FPO's. And also provide them with a Market Place where they can sell their products and get a fair amount of money against it. Also provide a query section where you can put your questions and get answers to your questions.

How can you be a part of this?

If you are a part of an FPO then you can register with us and sell your products through us. If you are a farmer then you can get all the information regarding FPO's and subsidies that you can get from government. If you are a buyer then you can login with us and buy from the FPO's.
